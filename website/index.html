About Us

A perfect workplace for all Freelancers

Our Motivation

There is at least something for everyone but for freelancers choice is limited. With that motivationa and purpose, we have tried to create a place where freelancers can upload their content, design and work similar to Instagram. And the hiring companies can review the same work before hiring them. This is what Instagram is to influencers and LinkedIn is to professionals. The freelancer can apply for a job based on rating of a company. The rating would be determined by a number of factors like maximum searches, maximum number of freelancers worked for the company.

  • 1. Getting hired based on your skills
  • 2. Tokenise your content
  • 3. Rating based Company
